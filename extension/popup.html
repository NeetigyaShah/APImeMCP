0 steps captured
What this does: when you click Record, it asks Chrome's permission to read data on the current site only, then captures your clicks/typing/navigation there. A small floating panel appears on the page itself (this popup closes as soon as you click elsewhere - that's normal browser behavior) so you can watch step count and Stop & Save without reopening this popup. On Stop, it also reads that site's cookies (to reproduce your session on replay) and sends everything to your local APImeMCP server (http://127.0.0.1:3000) — nothing leaves your machine.
